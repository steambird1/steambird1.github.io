Welcome to RATSC

RATSC, the official transportation accident and incident investigation organization in Regule Aire, has been safeguarding transportation safety in Regule Aire since its creation.

Independent Objective Investigations

RATSC guarantees that our investigation is independent and objective in order to provide the most accurate safety recommendations to aviation and other transportation industry.

Tracking Potential Danger

RATSC conducts routine examinations to transportation equipment and workers to identify any potential danger in transportation industry.

Professional Aviation Team

RATSC involves professional aviation team in order to conduct professional investigation on aviation accident and incidents at the fastest speed.
