Qiuming Test Paper Series

Qiuming Test Paper Series is what we used to prepare for Hangzhou Senior High School Enterance Exam (Actually it's much more difficult than Hangzhou's).

Downloading link is provided at the bottom of the page. The paper and the answer is in Chinese except English subject.

What's the difference?

Qiuming Series Hangzhou Ones
The full mark of math is 250 points and the difficulty is 0.45 The full mark of math is 120 points and the difficulty is 0.25
The full mark of science (including physics, chemistry and biology) is 320 points
and the difficulty is 0.3		 The full mark of science is 160 points and the difficulty is 0.2
The full mark of society (including history and geography) is 120 points (coming soon)
and the difficulty is 0.25		 The full mark of society is 50 points and the difficulty is 0.2

* Note: Difficulty = (Total points - Average points of students) / Total points

What can you expect to learn from them?

  • How to deal with difficult questions (especially for exams in the next year)
  • How to allocate time well, have a clear mind and stay calm
  • How to prepare for allocation exam of senior high

What can't you learn from them

  • What actual questions in exams are like
  • How to do well in English

    (This is because the test paper of English subject doesn't satisfy the format of Hangzhou's)
