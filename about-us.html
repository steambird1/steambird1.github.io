Welcome

We’re the official transportation accident investigation organization in Regule Aire.

RATSC is established after the 13th Regule Aire Congress, in view of the rapid development of Regule Aire aviation. It has a central organization and sub-organization in several Sky Islands.

What Do We Have

Independent Investigation

RATSC is an independent organization, not affected by other aviation authorities, by Regule Aire laws.

Objective Investigations

RATSC aims to conduct objective investigations to find out the truth behind accidents and incidents.

Tracking Potential Danger

RATSC conducts routine examinations to transportation equipment and workers to identify any potential danger in transportation industry.

Cooperations

RATSC has cooperation with a number of organizations, including the Guardian Wing Corps and CAM (in Mitakihara).

Professional Aviation Team

RATSC involves professional aviation team in order to conduct professional investigation on aviation accident and incidents at the fastest speed.
