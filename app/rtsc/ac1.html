RATSC Website Eventually Goes Online

After a week's preparation, RATSC now finally owns its official website. Website will initially have 5 RATSC accident and incident investigation report and other news about RATSC.

The establishment of RATSC website will surely make it more convenient for aviation lovers in Regule Aire to track latest aviation news online.

RATSC has set up long-term plans to update its website. However, it is sure to be a long process.
